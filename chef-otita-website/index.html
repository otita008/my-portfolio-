Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking your private chef experience.

How does the private chef service work? +

Once you select a menu or request a custom curation, Chef Otita handles menu development and baseline preparation. On the event day, the chef finishes preparation and plates the courses live at your venue, giving you a restaurant-quality experience at home.

Do you provide the ingredients? +

Yes, all required fresh and quality ingredients needed for your selected menu are sourced carefully and included within the standardized operational packages.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or allergies? +

Absolutely. Please specify any allergies, vegan/vegetarian preferences, or health-related dietary restrictions in the booking form so we can adjust the ingredients safely.

Do you travel outside of Nairobi? +

Primary services cover Nairobi and immediate surrounding areas (such as Kitengela). Travel arrangements for locations further out can be discussed and customized upon request.
